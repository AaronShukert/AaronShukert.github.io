Given and two continuous function $f:[0,1] \xrightarrow{} \mathbb{R}$ and $g:[0,1]  \xrightarrow{} \mathbb{R}$, we define the scalar product of _f_ and _g_, denoted by $\langle f,g\rangle$, by the formula

$\begin{equation*}
    \int_{0}^{1} f(x)g(x)dx. 
\end{equation*}$
